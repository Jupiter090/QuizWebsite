Features

Try to these cards

Easy quiz creation

Easily create quizzes, just add questions, fill in the options, choose one correct answer for each question and when finished click publish quiz.

Quick quiz sharing

After publishing your quiz, you are given a public quiz ID, which you can share with others or you can just copy the link given to you at the same time.

Quiz storing

Once you have created a quiz you don't lose access to it, they are stored and you can see information about them in the application in the navigation menu.

Quiz editing

If you have already published your quiz and realised you made an mistake there isn't anything to worry about. You can edit them by just clicking the edit button in the navigation menu.
